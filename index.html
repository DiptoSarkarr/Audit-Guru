About Audit Guru

Detect security threats, prove compliance and increase IT team efficiency with IT audit software from Audit Guru.

More and more organizations, regardless of size or industry, are recognizing the value of conducting regular internal and external IT audits. The benefits are many: IT auditing can help you improve security, pass compliance audits and optimize IT operations. But all too often, the process is far more cumbersome and time-consuming than it needs to be. Learn how Audit Guru software can ease your auditing burden and help you achieve your goals with far less effort.

Risk Assessment

Identify data and infrastructure security gaps, such as a large number of directly assigned permissions or too many inactive users, and take corrective actions to reduce your attack surface area.

Data Discovery

Protect your most critical assets first by pinpointing sensitive data across your file storages and regularly reviewing who has access to it and how it is used.

Reports

Quickly get the information required by management, auditors and other stakeholders instead of struggling with scripts, log files and spreadsheets.

Improve Detection

Spot malicious insiders and compromised accounts with a comprehensive view of all anomalous activity alerts triggered by an individual, along with the user’s associated cumulative risk score.
